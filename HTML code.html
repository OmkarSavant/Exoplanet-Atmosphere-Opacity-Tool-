Exoplanet Atmosphere Interpolator

Enter the pressure (bar) and temperature (Kelvin) that has been found in planetary atmospheres. The most abundant elements for that pressure and temperature will be presented in a bar graph and table. A graph of the wavelength v. opacity for that pressure and temperature will be shown, solely for the most abundant elements in our database. Our tool can only interpolate pressures that fall between 0.000001 bar - 10 bar and temperatures that fall between 75K - 4000K. This program runs fastest on Chrome, and may take longer on Safari and Firefox. Just enter your pressure and temperature! Tip: Use fullscreen for optimal performance and press the "Refresh Page" button to enter another set of values.
